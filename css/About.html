About Healing Rain Ministry

Our vision is to see lives transformed by the power of God's healing presence.

Our Mission and Vision

Healing Rain Ministry was founded on the belief that God desires to bring holistic healing – spiritual, emotional, and physical – to His children. We are committed to creating a space where individuals worldwide can access prayer, spiritual resources, and a supportive community.

Our vision is to be a global beacon of hope, empowering believers and seekers alike to experience the tangible presence of God, fostering deep faith, and witnessing miraculous transformations.

What We Believe

  • The Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God.
  • There is one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • In the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His vicarious and atoning death, His bodily resurrection, His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and His personal return in power and glory.
  • In the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life.
  • In the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.
